About Me:


I have a personal notion that art and technology are one in the same. Both feel like creative and exciting adventures I undertake at the start of every project. I first found my love for art and design before quickly becoming enamored with the endless potential and exponential growth of technology. Together they become a very powerful platform. Each project has it's own aesthetic and essence; crafting that combination is something I thrive on.


I was born in San Francisco bay, but shortly moved to the great Midwest. Here I live and grow with the multitude of facets of our community, drawing inspiration from the art, music, and many cultures around me. I've always focused on fostering human connection and find those connections drive me to continually make great collaborations and spectacular work.
